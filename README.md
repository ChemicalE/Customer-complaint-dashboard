# Customer Complaint Dashboard – Cumulus Finance

**Project Overview**

This project focuses on analysing customer complaint data for Cumulus Finance and building an interactive dashboard in Excel to help identify trends, operational issues, and service performance.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly monitor complaint categories, resolution performance, and patterns in customer issues. The goal of the project was to transform raw complaint data into meaningful insights that support better decision-making and improved customer experience.

**Objectives**

Analyse customer complaint trends.

Identify the most common sources of complaints.

Evaluate complaint resolution performance.

Create an interactive dashboard for easy exploration of complaint data.

**Tools & Technologies**

**Microsoft Excel**

Excel Functions (data cleaning and transformation)

Pivot Tables

Charts

Slicers for interactive filtering

**Key Insights**

Account management and payment processing issues were identified as the primary drivers of customer complaints.

97% of customer complaints were resolved within the required timeframe, indicating strong operational efficiency in customer support.

The dashboard provides a clear overview of complaint trends, helping management quickly identify areas that require attention.
